About Chris Hall

Chris Hall is a highly regarded financial advisor committed to delivering personalized strategies that help clients achieve financial security and build lasting legacies. With more than 26 years in the industry, he leads Hall Financial Advisors while serving as a Senior Advisor at Raymond James.

A graduate of The Ohio State University with dual majors in Business Administration, Chris holds advanced certifications including CPFA™ (Certified Plan Fiduciary Advisor) and CEPA™ (Certified Exit Planning Advisor), enabling expert guidance in fiduciary planning and business transitions.

Current Projects

Free Flow Trading Platform

As Senior Financial Advisor at Soros Fund Management LLC through the Free Flow platform, I am leading the development and management of an innovative online trading and investment solution. Free Flow is a user-friendly platform designed to grow wealth through diversified markets including Forex (with leverage up to 1:500 and tight spreads), Commodities (like gold and oil), Stocks (global equities with commission-free trading), and Cryptocurrencies (over 500 coins including Bitcoin and Ethereum, with advanced security features).

Key features include expert-managed discretionary accounts, real-time market data, customized investment plans for maximum returns, monthly performance reports, and 24/7 support. The platform boasts over $500M in assets under management, 10,000+ active investors, and a proven track record of 45%+ average annual returns—all secured with bank-level encryption and regulated by international authorities.

Built with Python on a scalable infrastructure, Free Flow emphasizes transparency with no hidden fees, making it ideal for both novice and experienced investors. This project represents my commitment to leveraging technology for accessible, high-performance financial growth.
